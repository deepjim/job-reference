To play the game, type the numbers shown on the screen in either horizontal or vertical order and press Enter.

Relax mode

This is non-timed. 

Normal mode

Similar to Relax mode, but it has the time limit of two minutes.  Moreover, some numbers would suddenly change. 

Challenge mode

Some numbers will illuminate and change after a while. Clearing such numbes yields bonus. FEVER bar gradually fills when you gain scores. when the FEVER bar is completely filled. Enter “FEVER” mode by pressing “0” where you can clear contiguous numbers in all directions.
